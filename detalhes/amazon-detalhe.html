This is a new feature for a platform of our choice - everything it’s possible. Maybe we were inspired by Xmas season. Our goal became to work on the Amazon website and add value to the experience of buying gifts. This was a group exercise for the Digital Experience Design postgraduate course.

Research Plan

Benchmark SWOT analyzes Amazon’s site map User flow Identify Amazon’s design system

To help us understand how this GROUP GIFT works meet our super-duper organized friend

Anna will try it out

Let’s imagine

Anna checks the landing page to know more about this feature. She sees that it works in 4 steps, seems pretty easy.

After Anna sets up the Gift Panel with few details, and answer some questions about Elisa - so Amazon can give some gift suggestions - she can choose from those listed or add some more to the gift list.

A few days later, there is a most voted gift chosen by all the group. If everybody agrees Anna gives the order to check out.

In the checkout process, each one of the members will have the chance to write a personal note. Once everyone pays, Anna just needs to confirm everything is ok and the order is shipped.

Hurray!

Some conclusions

  • This group gift feature is well received by those who hear about it.
  • Amazon’s design system is very confusing. It was difficult to maintain consistency.
